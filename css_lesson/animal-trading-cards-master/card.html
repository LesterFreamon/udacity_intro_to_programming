Space Dog

Space Dogs have 18 muscles controlling their ears.

  • Scientific Name: Choya Boya
  • Height:
    • Male: 20–23 inches (51–58 cm)
    • Female: 18–21 inches (46–54 cm)
  • Weight:
    • Male: 55–70 lbs (25–32 kg)
    • Female: 35–55 lbs (16–25 kg)
  • Label: Not-Too-Bright canine snuggle bug

Space dogs are a sub-breed of the Australian Shepherd and are identical to them in virtually every way. For that reason some scientists classify them as the same breed. The only apparent difference is the Space Dog's tendency to get their head stuck in the trash can. In fact, that is where their name comes from, as the bin resembles an astronaut helmet.
